GRILLED ASIAN CHICKEN

Great for last-minute company or a quick dinner by rounding it out with a baked potato and tossed salad.

This juicy and tender Grilled Asian Chicken recipe is smothered in a sweet and spicy Asian sauce. It’s the perfect Summer meal that takes less than 10 minutes of prep time! Asian Chicken is sticky, sweet and savoury everything you know and love about Asian food! An Asian Chicken Marinade infuses the chicken with flavour, and can be cooked on the grill, stove OR oven.

You can never have too many Asian chicken recipes, and this one is a great all rounder!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 4 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 slices fresh ginger root
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. In a small microwave-safe bowl, combine the soy sauce, oil, honey, ginger root, and garlic.
  2. Heat in microwave on medium for 1 minute, then stir.
  3. Heat again for 30 seconds, watching closely to prevent boiling.
  4. Place chicken breasts in a shallow dish. Pour soy sauce mixture over, and set aside to marinate for 15 minutes.
  5. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat.
  6. Drain marinade from chicken into a small saucepan.
  7. Bring to a boil, and simmer over medium heat for 5 minutes. Set aside for basting.
  8. Lightly oil the grill grate. Cook chicken on the prepared grill 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until juices run clear.
  9. Baste frequently with remaining marinade. Chicken will turn a beautiful golden brown.
  10. Serve immediately.
  11. Enjoy!
